About Us

The Fighting Gobies have been playing competitive ultimate in the Ohio Valley region since 1995. In the last twenty years, we have developed into three teams: Division I men’s, Division I women’s, and Developmental men’s. We travel around the Ohio Valley and the East Coast to participate in tournaments, as well as host our own annual tournaments, North Coast and CWRUL.

Both DI teams regularly qualify for and place well at Regionals. The teams have had enormous success in recent years. In 2016 the Division I men's team qualified for nationals for the first time and in 2018, the Division I women's team played in two games to go at Regionals.

As this disc sport has continued to grow in popularity and recognition across the country, we have grown as a team. We run high intensity practices and push each other to succeed, but also spend our down time as the closest friends that you can find. Our club is open to all CWRU students (undergrad and graduate), faculty, and staff. Join us at any of our recruitment events or contact us!
